Welcome to our Climate Dashboard!

Using extreme weather data from the National Climatic Data Center and National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration, this dashboard displays interative visualizations of maximum temperatures, maximum precipitation, maximum snowfall, and maximum snow depth recorded by weather stations across the globe from 1980-2009.
